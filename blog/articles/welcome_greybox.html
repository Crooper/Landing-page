Why we are building Greybox

Welcome to Greybox!

With Greybox, we embark on a journey to make thinking and concepting in 3D more accessible.

We firmly believe in the power of 3D to bridge the digital and physical worlds. It enables us to create objects and experiences that we can interact with and immerse ourselves in, offering unparalleled creative freedom - a tool for building worlds.

Yet, despite being our most technologically advanced design tool, or maybe because of it, 3D design is still considered a niche expertise that is beyond the capabilities of the many, even within industries that heavily rely on it.

This is where Greybox comes in.

We are creating the fastest concepting tool to take ideas to 3D scenes in minutes.

Our experience relies on 3 pillars:

  • Accessibility: without any prior knowledge, anyone can jump in Greybox and start concepting a scene in 3D space from their laptop.
  • Speed: Greybox enables you to start iterating on 3D concepts within minutes.
  • Collaboration: Greybox brings the 3D design process in a collaborative cloud-space where entire teams can view, review, and work together.

Please join us in our mission to unlock 3D thinking and don’t hesitate to reach out out with feedback, suggestions, or if you just want to talk 3D design! ☕
